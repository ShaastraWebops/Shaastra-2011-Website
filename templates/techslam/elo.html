About Elo

The system of rating photos during the Shaastra techslam will be similar to the Elo rating used to rank professional chess players. The algorithm has been slightly tweaked and entirely developed by the Shaastra Webops team. The rating of a photo in the techslam will be determined by its performance against other photos, and the relative ratings of the photos that it beats or loses to. For example, if a photo with a low rating were to beat one with a much higher rating, the rating of the former would increase drastically, and that of the latter would drop drastically. But if the reverse were to happen, i.e., a highly rated photo were to beat a much lower one, their ratings would not be affected as much. The algorithm is designed to take into account the dynamic nature of the ratings, and rank and re-rank photos accordingly. For a more detailed mathematical explanation of the working of the Elo algorithm, please visit Wikipedia
