About Techslam

Ever been obsessed with science to the point that you believed that you have “an eye for technology” if something like that exists? Ever wished you could put up on display something so everyday but something you find incredibly, mind-bogglingly tech quotient filled? Well, here is your chance to slam your way to fame!

Cause, we bring to you Techslam, an online photo contest that showcases the brilliance of technology from normal, day-to-day sightings and ideas. Here, what counts is not how

wonderful the photo is or how talented the photographer is but just the idea that is being lifted out of its mundane, ordinary setting for everyone to notice (and rate, of course, what’s more fun than that?) what you have tried to translate.

So all that you need to be a part of this event is an idea and some creativity, just enough to make it sell. Think you have the super-spotting-tech-concepts-and-clicking/drawing/ sketching power? Well, you know what to do. Oh yes, we know, this one is going to be some slam!

Theme of Techslam

The concept of Techslam is to provide a platform for science and technology enthusiasts to showcase their talent in an unconventional manner. The contestants are expected to capture their imagination and innovation in just a ‘click’. It is vital for all the photographs/designs to have a technological connect. Pictures with aspects of green/ground-breaking/cutting edge technology are encouraged. So, post your image and see if it goes vintage!
