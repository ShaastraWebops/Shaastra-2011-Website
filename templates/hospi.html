         On behalf of the entire Shaastra team, we would like to extend a warm welcome to you and hope that you cherish each and every moment of your stay at the beautiful campus of IIT Madras. Shaastra, the annual technical festival of IIT Madras, is undeniably one of the largest technical festivals of India which provides a unique platform for young minds to stretch their imaginations to the limit and exhibit their skills across a multitude of technically challenging, arduous and thought provoking events traversing all fields of engineering. After a decade long tryst with destiny, Shaastra has evolved into a bigger celebration of technological grandeur, alongside an attempt to provide its enthusiastic participants a better environment to develop their skills and thoughts to make this world a better place to live in.



         We at the Hospitality Team shall leave no stone unturned to ensure that your stay with us is enjoyable and comfortable. In order to enable us to assist you in the best possible manner, we would request you to register your college contingent at the Hospitality web portal as early as possible. Shaastra 2011 is all about 'Inspiring the Future'. It is the Youth, the Visionaries, that form the framework of this Shaastra, with nothing holding you back from shaping your imagination, your innovation or the competitive spirit vested in you. We work to make you smile.

Q.1 What should I do after coming to the campus?

»         Outstation participants who have registered for accommodation are requested to report to the control room where the required formalities involving checking of documents, allotment of rooms and mess registration will be finished. We shall not be providing accommodation for local participants. All the participants who are accommodated at IITM are supposed to collect their Shaastra ID Cards from the Hospitality team.

Q.2 What should I bring with me?

»         1.It is mandatory for all participants or company representatives to carry college IDs or Company IDs

»         2.If your accommodation is confirmed, you are required to bring a hard copy of the SAAR which will be mailed to you.

»         3.Each participant is also supposed to carry bonafide certificates issued by their respective colleges.

»         4.Mattresses and pillows shall be provided to you if your accommodation is confirmed. Participants will have to bring their own things which they require for the events in which they are participating in.

Q.3 What about the eating facilities?

»         A variety of food stalls shall be set up in the heart of the festive area which will cater to all your food requirements. Apart from that, there are numerous other eating outlets in and around the campus like Cafe Coffee Day, Tiffany's Tiffin centre, Campus Cafe, Basera restaurant, Gurunath canteen to name a few.

Q.4 What about the hospital facility?

»         There is an institute Hospital located close to Gajendra Circle. In case you fall ill, you are advised to report to us at control room. We shall make appropriate arrangements for you to be treated at the Institute hospital.

Q.5 What about the security facilities?

»         IITM campus has a vigilant and round-the-clock security service. To ensure the safety of the participants, there will be additional security guards in hostels in order to avoid thefts and other mishaps.

Q.6 What about certificates and prize money?

»         For competitive events, certificates will be given only to the teams coming first, second and third. For major events all participants qualifying to the final round will be issued certificates. Participation Certificates will not be given. Instead, a general letter of participation will be given to the participants. For more information, visit the certificate policy tab in the Shaastra website. The cash prize amount is event-specific and details can be found out by visiting the events page on the Shaastra website.

Q.7 I still have unanswered queries, what should I do?

»         You can make use the helpdesk facility in the User-portal and any queries related to hospitality can be directed to the hospitality queries section. In addition to that, you can contact us by mailing to

A.  From Chennai Central

»         Hire an auto/taxi to IIT Madras. You can catch institute busses from the IIT Madras main gate to control room

»         You can catch a bus just outside the Chennai Central. 18B and 19S are the direct busses to IITM (Get down at Gandhi Mandapam/IITM). From the gate there are IITM busses to the hostels, every 20 minutes

»         The fare from Chennai Central will cost you approximately Rs.120 for auto and Rs. 200 for a taxi till the Main Gate. For your convenience, fix the rate before boarding the auto.

B.   From Egmore Station:

»         Catch 23C which heads directly to IITM. From the main gate there are IITM busses to the hostels, every 20 minutes

»         The auto and taxi fares from Egmore are approximately Rs.100 and Rs.150 respectively.

C. From Airport:

»         Hire an auto/taxi to IIT Madras. Inside the campus, get down at the Hospitality Control Room

»         Or catch PP21 directly to institute or 21G to Gandhi Mandapam which is at a stone throw’s distance from out gate. From the main gate there are IITM busses to the hostels, every 20 minutes.

D. From CMBT:

»         Catch Bus number 23M or 5E which come directly to institute. From the main gate there are IITM busses to the hostels, every 20 minutes.

Upon reaching IIT

»         Look out for the directions, which we shall position at appropriate locations within the campus.

»         The hospitality team would be based out of a Control Room. Boys should report to Mahanadi Hostel, and girls to Sharavati Hostel.

»         If you are not coming by taxi/auto, board the IIT bus at the Main gate, which plies every 20 mins, and disembark in front of our control room.

»         Nominal charges for accommodation facility will be collected here depending on the number of days you wish to avail this facility.

»         Kindly note that you will be required to share the accommodation.

»         Requests for individual rooms or any other complaints in this regard will not be entertained.

»         There are a variety of canteens inside the campus which provide food at reasonable rates.
