·         Look out for the directions, which we shall position at appropriate locations within the campus

·         The hospitality team would be based out of a Control Room. You are required to report here as soon as you arrive.

·         If you are not coming by taxi/auto, board the IIT bus at the Main gate, which plies every 20 mins, and disembark in front of our control room.

·         Nominal charges for accommodation facility will be collected here depending on the number of days you wish to avail this facility.

·         Kindly note that you will be required to share the accommodation.

·         Requests for individual rooms or any other complaints in this regard will not be entertained. Also, apart from our mess facility, there are a variety of canteens inside the campus which provide food at reasonable rates.
